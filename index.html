About Menara Technip

Menara Technip is a GBI-Gold Certified Green Building accredited by Green Building Malaysia Sdn Bhd. This 16-storey commercial building is located at Jalan Inai, Kuala Lumpur. It currently serves as an extension from the existing Wisma Technip to create a larger workspace for Technip’s growing workforce.

・Property Info・

  • Location : Jalan Inai
  • Storey : 16
  • Lettable Area: 200,000 sq. ft.
  • Block : 1
  • GBI certification: Gold
  • Completed: October 2014

・Special Features・

Menara Technip, a Grade A commercial development, awarded a Green Building Index Gold certification, was completed in the third quarter of 2014.
